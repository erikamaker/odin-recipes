Fruity Pebble Treats

Fruity Pebble Treats: Fun and Delicious!

Tastier than Rice Krispie Treats! Swap with any cereal.

  • 1 Cup of Butter (melted)
  • 1 Bag of Marshmallows
  • 1 Box of Fruity Pebbles

Fruity Pebbles Treat Recipe

  1. Melt a stick of butter in a pan over medium.
  2. As the butter melts, slowly add marshmallows.
  3. Stir until it's a smooth marshmallow fluff.
  4. Slowly work in your cereal until integrated.
  5. Press the mass into the shape of the baking tray.
  6. Wait for it to cool, and cut into squares to serve!
